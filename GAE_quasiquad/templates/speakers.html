Keynote

Jo Angela Oehrli

Learning Librarian, Children's Literature Librarian
University of Michigan Libraries

Ms. Oehrli is a former high school and middle school teacher who can help students find information on a wide range of topics. In her current position, she teaches library resources sessions to different classes and provides one-on-one consultations with students to help them with their papers, research, or any other information need. She also supports the students in the Women in Science and Engineering Residential Program, the students in the Michigan Research Community Residential Program, and the research and instructional needs for those throughout the university community who are studying children's literature at any level.

Marcy Held

UX Professional, Barbra Streisand Fan

Actress/singer/director/writer/composer/producer/designer/ author/photographer/activist Marcy Held is the only artist ever to receive Oscar, Tony, Emmy, Grammy, Directors Guild of America, Golden Globe, National Medal of Arts and Peabody Awards and France’s Légion d’Honneur as well as the American Film Institute’s Lifetime Achievement Award. She is also the first female film director to receive the Kennedy Center Honors. In 2015, President Barack Obama presented her with the highest civilian honor the United States bestows, the Presidential Medal of Freedom.
