Submissions Are Now Open!

The student ALA and SAA groups are accepting submissions for our annual QuasiCon information conference. Proposals from all interest areas are welcome and encouraged. For details visit the submission form (ADD LINK)

QuasiCon 2018 Theme

This year's theme is "Discovery & Distortion". Some suggested areas of interest within this theme include diversity, accessibility, community programming, makerspaces, technology, non-traditional collections/spaces/services and libraries/archives outside of traditional spaces.
