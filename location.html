Location

QuasiCon 2018 will take place on Saturday, February 10, 2018 at the Rackham School of Graduate Studies located 915 E. Washington St on the University of Michigan campus.

Parking

We suggest that attendees park at the Liberty Square Parking Structure (510 E. Washington), a short walk to the Rackham Graduate Building (915 E. Washington Street). This lot charges a flat $3 exit fee for parking on Saturdays.
